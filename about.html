Atlas Space Corp and our Mission

At Atlas Space Corp, we find out what our national security is looking to develop and provide solutions by developing our teams skills and improving our assets. Our efforts support the development of our homeland, the United States of America. We contract various projects in order to ensure strong defense for the United States.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing flight boundaries.
  • Sustainability: Designing for a cleaner and stronger country.
  • Safety: Prioritizing lives and reliability.
  • Collaboration: Building the future as a team.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

Colby Burham

Alaskan native Colby Burham founded Atlas Space Corp with over 10+ years of aerospace and mechanical engineering experience, serving for the United States Air Force. A visionary leader and expert in sustainable propulsion, Colby combines passion and impressive expertise to lead the company toward an incredible future in aviation.
